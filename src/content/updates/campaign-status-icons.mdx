There are a lot of moving parts inside a campaign. Sending statuses, errors,
bottlenecks, sending windows, delays, deadlines and so on. Finding the cause
of a problem can sometimes be tricky so how about we make it easier to see,
at a glance, what the overall status of a campaign is for each of these
dimensions?
